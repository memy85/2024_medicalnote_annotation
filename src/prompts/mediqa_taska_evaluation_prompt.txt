In this task, you'll be reviewing a section summary derived from a brief patient-doctor conversation. Please consider coherence, completeness, naturalness in your evaluation. Be sure to read these instructions attentively, and refer back to this guide as necessary during your review.

Evaluation Criteria:

Coherence (1-5): Reflects the logical consistency and flow of the summary.
Completeness (1-5): Measures how well the summary covers the details from the conversation.
Naturalness (1-5): Assesses how natural the language of the summary sounds.
Steps for Evaluation:

Start by thoroughly reading the patient-doctor dialogue. Pinpoint the crucial details and points.
Next, read the summary and compare it to the dialogue. Check if the summary fully and accurately covers the main points and details, and if the summary is naturally written.
Assign a score for each criterion (coherence, completeness, naturalness) on a scale from 1 to 5, where 1 signifies the lowest quality and 5, the highest.
